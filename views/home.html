Securely share data

Sharelock allows you to share data easily and securely. Simply type a secret and the e-mail or twitter handle of the person(s) you want to share this with and an encrypted link (URL) will be generated.

While the URL can be shared with anyone, intended recipients will have to prove their identity by logging in using their Google, Facebook, Microsoft or Twitter account.

How it works or fork the repository from

Data to share

Enter a secret you want to share.

Who to share it with

Enter one or more e-mail addresses, Twitter handles or even an entire e-mail domain.

Sharelocked data

An encrypted and signed link containing your secret data will be generated.

You can share this link with anynone using any messaging application but recipients will have to prove their identity by logging in using their Google, Facebook, Microsoft or Twitter account to access the data.

For quick sharing, use the Mac OS App

The Mac OS app provides a convenient way of creating sharelock URLs.

Use it

Create Sharelocks from a native Mac OS app, an Android app or the Web app.
